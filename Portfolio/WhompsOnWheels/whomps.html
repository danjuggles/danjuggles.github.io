Whomps On Wheels

Portable, Sustainable Entertainment

Welcome to Whomps on Wheels – the ultimate fusion of mobility, sustainability, and high-quality event production. Our compact, yet impressively equipped, stage stands 2.2m tall, 1.8m wide, and 2.5m long. Thoughtfully designed for easy transport, it seamlessly accommodates a variety of venues and packs down efficiently when the show's over.

Sustainable by Design

Whomps on Wheels isn't just about great sound and vibrant light shows, it's about eco-conscious entertainment. We've made sustainability a core part of our design, using biodegradable insulation for all wiring, integrating solar panels for an efficient, renewable power source, and used scrap/salvaged material wherever possible. We carbon offset the entire build, as well as the ongoing useage. This battery-operated, sustainable stage not only lowers your carbon footprint but also never skips a beat.

High-Powered Sound & Dynamic Lighting

Outfitted with an impressive 8kW sound system, Whomps on Wheels delivers unparalleled sound clarity, creating an outstanding audio experience for your event. The integrated dynamic LED light show, customizable via VDJ software, adds another layer of spectacle, whether you want a predefined sequence or to control it live for that extra personal touch.

Smart Mobility for Any Terrain

Moving the stage is easy. Thanks to our dedicated smartphone application, you can remotely control Whomps on Wheels with the utmost ease. The stage’s electric motors enable driving and steering, capable of handling off-road terrain, fields, and more, making setup a breeze no matter where your event is.

Unrivaled Design and Engineering

The eye-catching design and meticulous engineering of Whomps on Wheels set it apart from any other portable stage. We've devoted considerable time and passion to crafting this versatile, robust, and aesthetically pleasing product, designed to make your event truly unique.

Perfect for Small Event Organizers

Whomps on Wheels is ideally suited for small event organizers who value sustainability without compromising on quality, functionality or cool factor. We believe we've achieved this balance with Whomps on Wheels - a mobile stage that truly aligns with the ethos of environmental stewardship, superior quality, and innovative design.
